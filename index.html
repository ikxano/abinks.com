Frequently Asked Questions

Is Abinks really free for students?

Yes! Abinks is 100% free for students. We make money by charging property managers a small fee when you move in, not from you.

How soon can I find housing?

Many students find and secure housing within 48 hours of signing up. For popular areas, we recommend starting your search 2-3 months in advance.

What if I don't like my matched properties?

Our matching algorithm improves with your feedback. If you don't like your initial matches, simply update your preferences and we'll find new options.

How does the roommate matching work?

Our compatibility algorithm uses a 20-point questionnaire to match you with potential roommates based on lifestyle, study habits, and personal preferences.
