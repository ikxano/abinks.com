Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Dwello cost?

Dwello is completely free for students to use. We make our revenue through partnerships with property managers and universities.

How are listings verified?

Our team verifies each property through a combination of virtual tours, document checks, and landlord verification processes.

When should I start looking for housing?

We recommend starting 3-4 months before your desired move-in date, especially in competitive markets near popular universities.

Can I find housing if I'm studying abroad?

Yes! Dwello is perfect for international students or those studying abroad. Our verification process ensures you can trust listings from afar.
