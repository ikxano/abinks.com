About Us

Abinks, a subsidiary of Inkauxano, is dedicated to creating innovative solutions for student housing challenges. Founded in 2019, we've quickly established ourselves as a leader in developing tools that help students find affordable and suitable accommodation.

Our mission is to simplify the housing search process while maintaining affordable prices. We believe in sustainable practices and ethical business operations that benefit the student community.
