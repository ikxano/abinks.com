Accessibility

At Dwello, we are committed to ensuring that our student housing finder app is accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive experience that allows all users to easily find, compare, and book their ideal apartments. Here are the measures we take to ensure accessibility:

Accessibility Features

  • Screen Reader Compatibility:

    Our app is designed to be compatible with screen readers, allowing visually impaired users to navigate and interact with the platform effectively.

  • Keyboard Navigation:

    Users can navigate through the app using keyboard shortcuts, ensuring that those who cannot use a mouse can still access all features.

  • Text Alternatives:

    We provide text alternatives for all non-text content, such as images and diagrams, to ensure that users with visual impairments can understand and interact with the content.

  • High Contrast Mode:

    Our app offers a high contrast mode to assist users with low vision in reading text and distinguishing elements on the screen.

  • Resizable Text:

    Users can adjust the text size to suit their preferences, making it easier for those with visual impairments to read content.

Continuous Improvement

We are dedicated to continuously improving the accessibility of our app. We regularly review and enhance accessibility features based on user feedback and advancements in technology.

Dwello is committed to compliance with applicable accessibility laws and regulations, including the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG).
